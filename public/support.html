RUSSELL FTD — Support

RUSSELL FTD is a simple, private messaging app: real-time chats, group chats, photo sharing, 24-hour status updates, and voice & video calls.

Frequently asked questions

How do I create an account?
Open the app, tap "New here? Create an account", choose a username and password, and you're in. No phone number or email needed.

How do I start a chat?
Go to the Contacts tab and tap any user. Everyone who registers appears there automatically.

How do I make a call?
Open a chat and tap the phone icon (voice) or camera icon (video). The app will ask for microphone/camera permission the first time.

How do I post a status?
Go to the Status tab and tap the pencil button. Statuses disappear after 24 hours.
