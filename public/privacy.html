Privacy Policy

RUSSELL FTD — Last updated: 5 July 2026

RUSSELL FTD ("the App") is a messaging application. This policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

  • Account information: a username, a display name, and a password. The password is stored only in hashed (scrambled) form and cannot be read by anyone, including us.
  • Messages and media: text messages, photos, and status updates you send are stored on our server so they can be delivered to your recipients. Status updates are automatically deleted after 24 hours.
  • Call data: voice and video calls are connected directly between devices (peer-to-peer). Call audio and video are never stored on our server. The server only relays the brief technical handshake needed to start a call, which is deleted within 90 seconds.

What we do NOT collect

  • No phone number, email address, or contacts list is required or accessed.
  • No location data.
  • No advertising identifiers, tracking, or analytics.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share any user data with third parties.

Device permissions

  • Microphone: used only during voice and video calls that you start or accept.
  • Camera: used only during video calls and when you choose to attach a photo.
  • Photo library: accessed only when you choose a photo to send.

Data retention and deletion

Messages remain stored until removed by normal system limits. Status updates are deleted automatically after 24 hours. To request deletion of your account and all associated data, contact us at the address below and we will remove it within 30 days.

Children

The App is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Security

All communication between the App and the server uses HTTPS encryption. Passwords are hashed with an industry-standard algorithm (scrypt).

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update this page and change the date at the top.
